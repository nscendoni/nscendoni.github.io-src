How to use X11 via ssh and sudo

Published: ven 04 dicembre 2015
By Nicola Scendoni

In Linux.

xauth list |grep \:$(echo $DISPLAY|cut -d ":" -f2|cut -d "." -f1) > /tmp/xauth
chmod 666 /tmp/xauth
sudo su – [new user]
cat /tmp/xauth |xargs -l xauth add
